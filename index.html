Luo, Kelin 骆 柯琳

I'm a postdoc at the Department of Mathematics and Computer Science, Eindhoven University of Technology (TU/e), led by Prof. Frits Spieksma. My research interests include online algorithms and approximation algorithms.

In June 2019, I defended my Ph.D. thesis entitled "Online car-sharing problem with advance bookings" under the supervision of Prof. Yinfeng Xu in Xi'an Jiaotong University. From September 2017 till September 2018, I was a visiting Ph.D. student at the Department of Informatics of University of Leicester under the supervision of Prof. Thomas Erlebach. In 2014, I obtained my BSc in Economics and Management (Double degree) from Northwest University (China).

Check my Google Scholar and DBLP for a more up-to-date publication list.
